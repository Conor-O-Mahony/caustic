Some music genre's I find particularly interesting which will be explored in this site include

  • No Wave

    Emerged in New York City during the late 1970s; took the ethos of Punk Rock but discarded melody and catchiness, including instead extreme noise, distortion, feedback, angular rhythms, stream-of-consciousness lyrics, and more

  • Goth Rock

    Dark atmosphere and gothic imagery, featuring synthesizers and moderate use of chorus and echo effects.

  • Black Metal

    Typified by highly distorted, trebly, tremolo-picked guitars, blast beats, double kick drumming, shrieked vocals, and raw, underproduced sound that often favors atmosphere over technical skills and melody.
